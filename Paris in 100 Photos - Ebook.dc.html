Introduction
The city, one frame at a time.

There are a hundred Parises folded into one, and this book makes no claim to settle which is the true one. It simply looks — patiently, at street level — and lets the city answer in light.

The photographs gathered here were made over many seasons and at every hour: zinc rooftops under rain, the long gold of a summer quay, the blue minute before the lamps come on.

Turn the page slowly. Paris was never in a hurry to be understood.
